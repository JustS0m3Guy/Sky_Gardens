HEARD, BUT UNSEEN ENEMY!
Constant exposure to noise at 65 decibels or higher can lead to physical and mental health problems. The traffic on a busy road causes a noise level of around 80 decibels. The music in a disco or at a rock concert reaches up to 110 decibels, an aircraft engine around 120 decibels.

THE TRUTH ABOUT ELECTRIC SCOOTERS!
Contrary to the promises made by the manufacturers, electric scooters do not reduce traffic, because users do not use them instead of the conventional means of transport, but in addition. Since they also have to be collected and charged every night, their ecological balance is poor.
