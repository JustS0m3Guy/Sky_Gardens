NOT TOO FAR FROM US!
Did you know? When it is very hot, even trees can sweat to survive. They release water through the pores in their leaves. However, the more they sweat, the more they stop absorbing CO2.

CITY SPEEDSTER!
In a city, the fastest way to get around is usually by bicycle. The Traffic Club Germany (VCD) has calculated a comparison for Berlin: For example, it takes almost a quarter of an hour to cover four kilometres by bike, 26 minutes by bus and 23 minutes by car.
